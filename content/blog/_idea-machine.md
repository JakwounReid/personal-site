## The System

**Where ideas come from — check these regularly:**

1. **Work friction** — Something annoyed you, surprised you, or took longer than it should at NM. That's a post.
2. **Questions you Googled** — If you searched for something and the results were bad, you have a post to write.
3. **Things you explained to someone** — Slack message, conversation, DM. If you explained something, you can write it.
4. **Side project decisions** — Every non-obvious choice you make while building is a post (why you picked X over Y, what broke, what you'd do differently).
5. **Career moments** — Promotions, new skills, transitions, mistakes. People at earlier stages of your path want to read this.

**Rating system (use before starting a post):**

- ⭐ Idea only — don't start yet
- ⭐⭐ Idea + I know what I'd say — could write this weekend
- ⭐⭐⭐ High confidence + searchable — prioritize this one

**SEO check before writing:**
Ask yourself: "Would someone Google this?" If yes, what would they type?
Put that phrase in your title or first paragraph.
